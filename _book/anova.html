Chapter 8 One-Way ANOVA

Learning objectives for this lesson: - Write one-way ANOVA model - Define terms - state assumptions - interpret results - Interpret ANOVA table - Describe SSE, SST, MSE - F-statistic - degrees of freedom - understand how all of these interrelate - Understand how to compare mulitple group means how ANOVA is similar/different to t-tests - Understand partitioning of variation and coefficient of determination
